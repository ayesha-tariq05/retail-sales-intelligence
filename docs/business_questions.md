2. Profitability (Profitability Check)
- Which products have high sales but low profit?
- How does discount affect profit?
Insight (Profitability/Operations): The Furniture category has the lowest profit margin because it combines the highest discount rates (16.8%) with high shipping costs. It is highly likely that the current strategy of deep discounting is not sustainable, as it fails to account for the heavy logistics overhead.

3. Customer (Customer Value)
- Which customer segments are most valuable?
Insight (Customer): The Consumer segment is the primary profit driver, contributing nearly 50% of the total profit compared to Corporate and Home Office segments. Marketing efforts and loyalty initiatives should prioritize the Consumer segment to maintain and expand this high-value base.

5. Geography (Expansion Strategy)
- Which countries/regions should receive more investment?
Insight (Geography): The Central and North regions are the primary profit engines. Conversely, Southeast Asia and Canada are currently underperforming significantly, contributing less than 6% of the profit generated by the Central region. These regions require a "Market Audit" to determine if the low profit is due to low sales volume or high operational overhead.
